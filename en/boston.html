BOSTON
Landing page for club house
Design
November 2019
Behance

Club House Boston is an apartment with unique architecture and interiors, in which visible luxury is combined with comfort.

In the process of creating the project, a competitive analysis was carried out, a convenient structure was built and a visual concept was drawn in the style of minimalism.

The site page allows the user to familiarize themselves with the location and characteristics, evaluate the benefits and offers of the club house, and in the end leave a request by filling out the feedback form.

An adaptive version was also designed, through which the site is correctly displayed on all devices.
