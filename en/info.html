Hello!

I am Svetlana, UI/UX designer from Russia. I design sites in the style of minimalism.

In a few words

In design, I am attracted by such aspects as: aesthetics, functionality, attention to composition, meaningfulness and the absence of excessive decorativeness.

In 2020, I successfully completed a web design course at the UPROCK school under the leadership of Evgeny Kuzmin, an art director with more than 14 years of experience in this field, who is a member of the juru “Rating of Runet” and “G8”, winner in the nomination “The Best Website of the Year”.

Work process

My approach to design development is aimed at making projects not only beautiful, but also effective, convenient and easy to use.

Briefing

Before starting work, it is important to understand in a dialogue what the client wants to get as a result, find out his taste preferences regarding the visual part of the site, find out the features of a product or service, its target audience and needs.

Formulating clear requirements for a task usually leads to an excellent result.

Technical task

Based on the brief, I make a technical task in which the project requirements will be reflected, the site structure, a list of all pages with a detailed description.

After agreeing the technical task, I will begin the next stage.

Prototyping

At the prototyping stage, I will definitely perform a visual analysis — this process helps to create a website with a relevant and demanded design, choose modern solutions.

Then I work in detail the composition of the blocks of all project pages and their order, integrating text received from the client and photo / video content.

Visual concept

After agreeing on the prototype I start drawing a visual concept — the graphic idea of the future site, expressed using the sketches of key partitions.

I demonstrate the result to the client, if necessary, introduce edits.

Design of all site pages

After the concept is approved, I draw the final design of the entire site, demonstrating the result to the client to approve, if necessary, I make edits.

Adaptive design

I make adaptive versions for the most common permits so that the site is correctly displayed on all modern devices.

Preparing the layout for development

Transfer of a file that contains the design layout of all pages of the site and the UI Kit with a detailed description for further development.

For several years I work in a team with an excellent web developer, so a turnkey website option is possible.
