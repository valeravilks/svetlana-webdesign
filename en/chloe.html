CHLOE
Online store redesign
Design
December 2019
Behance

Chloe is a French fashion house specializing in clothes, shoes, accessories and perfumes.

The goal of this project is to update the look and structure of an existing online store.

The design was created in such a way as to focus the user's attention on the content and create a sense of elegance.

The catalog page is designed taking into account the convenience for site visitors. On the left side there is a button for filtering by style, type and color of the product, and on the right — a button for sorting by newness and price.

The product page contains all the necessary information, buttons for choosing the color and size of the product, adding it to the cart or to the wish list.

This is how the list of items in the cart looks like.

Next, a window appears for placing an order and payment.

The “Collections” page contains photos and videos from the show of the fashion house in the current season.

An adaptive version was also designed, through which the site is correctly displayed on all devices.
