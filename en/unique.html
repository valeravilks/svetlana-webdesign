UNIQUE
Digital agency site
Design
March 2020
Behance

Unique is an agency that specializes in branding, services and applications.

After conducting a competitive analysis, I created a stylish, minimalist design with a focus on studio work.

On the main page you can see the cover and the slogan of the agency, then the announcements of the internal pages (“About the company”, “Projects”, “Services”, etc.) with the possibility of switching to them.

The Projects page contains a list of the agency's work. On the left, there is a filter by category. When you click on a project, a page with its description opens.

Page "About the Company" with general information about the agency, directions of its work, clients, awards, team.

The “Services” page is designed so that the site user can familiarize himself with the agency's service, and then have the opportunity to go to the list of completed projects, taking into account the chosen direction.

The Contacts page maintains the overall style of the site.

An adaptive version was also designed, through which the site is displayed correctly on all devices.
