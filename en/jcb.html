JCB
Corporate website
Design
May 2020
Behance

JCB is a British company that manufactures loaders, excavators, tractors, dump trucks and similar equipment. Today JCB is one of the three world leaders in the production of construction equipment.

The purpose of my work was to create a new design of the existing site, which will emphasize the premium and status of the company.

On the main page you can see the announcements of the internal pages ("About the Company", "Products", "News") with the possibility of switching to them.

This is how the opened “Menu” of the site looks like.

The “Products” page allows the user to conveniently select the desired product category and familiarize himself with the company's assortment.

The section “About the Company” contains information about the company and its main stages of development.

On the "Career" page, you can find suitable vacancies and learn about the prospects for work in the company.

The Dealer Locator page maintains the overall style of the site, and easy navigation allows you to find the information you need.

"Company news" is represented by cards, by clicking on which the article page opens.

The "Contacts" page looks clean and premium at the same time.

An adaptive version was also designed, through which the site is displayed correctly on all devices.
