IBANEZ
Corporate website
Design
October 2020
Behance

Ibanez is one of the first Japanese musical instrument companies to make a name for itself all over the world.

This project is a new design of the corporate website of a popular guitar manufacturer. In it, I wanted to reflect my vision of the visual concept and convey a certain atmosphere of rock music with the help of bright headlines and a non-standard grid.

The main page contains announcements of internal pages with the possibility of a transition.

The main page contains announcements of internal pages with the possibility of a transition.

The catalog page maintains a minimalist style.

The Electric Guitars section contains a cover, a block with product features, signature guitars of the musicians and a line of series of the corresponding category.

On the page of a certain series of guitars, you can read its description, technical characteristics and go to the models of instruments.

The pages for acoustic and bass guitars are designed in dark colors.

Страницы артистов предлагают ознакомиться с биографией и фирменными гитарами музыкантов.
